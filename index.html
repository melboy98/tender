Some Local Treasure's

  • RAMLA BAY

    Ramla Bay is located at the bottom of a rich and fertile valley on the northern side of the island of Gozo. Gozo is one of the Maltese islands. The village of Xagħra, located on one of the hills of Gozo, overlooks this valley. It is about a forty-minute walk from Xagħra to the bay.

  • HONDOQ BAY

    Beautiful beach with a great view of Comino. Great for swimming and snorkeling. Surrounding area is also good for hiking.
