About Us

A Quiet Located, new renovated B&B in the Town of Kercem.

Our B&B is a new renovated Farmhouse in the Town of kercem. If winter or Summer our rooms are all Airconditioned, free WIFI in the whole house. If you're Fancing a swim but dont want to go to a beach, use our overflow Pool with sunbeds to make it a perfect relaxing area.

WE SERVE BREAKFAST

We serve Breakfast every day From 7.30 in the morning till 10.30am. So if you want to start your Day with a fresh orange juice and homemade Pastries, Dont sleep in. Dining Areas are provided inside near the living room whit T.V and a comfortable Sofa or outside near the Pool and BBQ Area. If you have any problems with allergies let us know and we will serve the Food according to your allergies. A small extra is that 2 evenings of the week we do a surprise evening, guess you have to come and see for yourself!

POOL AREA

Enjoy the Pool and its relaxing area 24hrs with from us provided Pool Towels. If you fancy cooking some meat on our Original Stone BBQ or enjoying the night with some wine on our Comfy Sofas, Its youre Holiday youre the King/Queen!
